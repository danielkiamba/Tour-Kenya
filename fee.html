Fees

Below are approximate costs for tours in Kisumu, Diani Beach, Mount Kenya, and Malindi: Kisumu: City Tours: Day tours exploring Kisumu's attractions, such as the Kisumu Museum and Dunga Hill Camp, typically range from $50 to $100 per person. Lake Victoria Excursions: Boat trips on Lake Victoria can cost between $30 and $70 per person, depending on the duration and services offered. Diani Beach: 3-Day Packages: A 3-day, 2-night package at Diani Beach resorts ranges from approximately Ksh 21,560 to Ksh 31,000 per person, depending on the accommodation and meal plan. OLANKASAFARIS.COM Water Sports: Activities like snorkeling and kite surfing are available at additional costs, varying by provider. Mount Kenya: 3-Day Climbing Packages: Premium packages for a 3-day, 2-night Mount Kenya climb start at around Ksh 174,895 per person. BOUNTIFULSAFARIS.COM Alternative Options: More budget-friendly trekking tours are available, with prices varying based on services and group size. Malindi: Beach Resort Packages: 3-day, 2-night stays at beach resorts in Malindi typically range from $200 to $500 per person, depending on the resort's luxury level and included amenities. Marine Park Tours: Excursions to Malindi Marine National Park, including snorkeling, usually cost between $40 and $100 per person. Please note that these prices are approximate and subject to change. It's advisable to contact tour operators directly for the most current rates and package details.
